Introduction

Capitals is a simple geography game for the iPhone written in Objective-C (of course!). Its completely open source and I wrote it as a learning experience.

The Math (and the Problem)

Capitals uses a list of 250 countries and is a true or false game. So, the chance of the answer being False is 249/250 → the chance of it being True is (250-249)/250 = 1/250. This means that its much faster to keep pressing the false button than it is to actually think whether the country and capitals are a match. As I said, I just wrote it as a learning experience.

It will not provide a significant challenge.
